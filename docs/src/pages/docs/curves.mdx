# Curve Catalog

Browse the built-in curves. Each curve has unique geometry, period, and behavior. Click any curve to see the details of its equation, parameters, and a live preview.

## Available Curves

### Standard

Some of the well-known and well-documented curves with easy to find mathematical definitions.
> Their commonality does not take away from their inherent beauty

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/rose3">Rose (n=3)</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Rose" /></span>
A 3-petaled rose curve with trifold symmetry. Also used as the Sarmal logo.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/rose5">Rose (n=5)</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Rose" /></span>
A 5-petaled rose curve with fivefold symmetry.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/rose52">Rose (n=5/2)</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Rose" /></span>
A 5-petaled rose with period 4π — a fractional exponent requires two full revolutions to close, producing elegant trail crossings through the center.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/epitrochoid7">Epitrochoid7</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Roulette" /></span>
A roulette tracing 7 lobes with an animated distance parameter that creates organic motion.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/astroid">Astroid</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Hypocycloid" /></span>
A hypocycloid with 4 sharp cusps, forming a star-like shape within a square.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/deltoid">Deltoid</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Hypocycloid" /></span>
A 3-cusped hypocycloid tracing a triangle-like curve.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/lissajous32">Lissajous 3:2</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Lissajous" /></span>
A figure-eight-like curve with a slowly drifting phase and live skeleton.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/lissajous43">Lissajous 4:3</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Lissajous" /></span>
A more complex Lissajous pattern with 4:3 frequency ratio, drifting phase, and live skeleton.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/epicycloid3">Epicycloid (n=3)</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Roulette" /></span>
A 3-pointed star shape created by a point on a rolling circle.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/lame">Lamé Curve</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Superellipse" /></span>
A superellipse that morphs continuously between diamond, circle, and squircle shapes.



### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/star">Star</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Fourier polar" /></span>
A 5-pointed star built from Fourier harmonics in polar coordinates.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/star4">Star (4-arm)</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Fourier polar" /></span>
A 4-pointed star using the same Fourier harmonic construction as Star.

### <span class='inline-flex gap-4 items-center'><Link href="/docs/curves/star7">Star (7-arm)</Link> {SEPARATOR_DOT} <Badge text="Fourier polar" /></span>
A 7-pointed star using the same Fourier harmonic construction as Star.
